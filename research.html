Achievements

  • The identification of variants in the LPHN3 gene that confer susceptibility to Attention Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) and substance use disorder, double the risk of developing ADHD, and predict response to stimulant medication and severity of symptoms.

  • Identified that a common genetic network involving the LPHN3 gene and the NCAM1-DRD2-TTC12 gene cluster underlies substance use disorders and disruptive or externalizing disorders.

  • Development of the statistical, computational and analytical framework for the functional analysis of genetic variants in the SIX3 gene using zebrafish, and the genetic analysis of mutations in the NODAL signalling pathway.

  • Development and implementation of a statistical strategy that allowed the identification of genes modifying the age of onset in patients with Alzheimer’s disease.

  • Identification of age of onset modifier genes in familial and sporadic Alzheimer’s disease.
